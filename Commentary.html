Did hospitals bring this on themselves
I dont shortchange the stupidity of the republican establishment, nor do I ignore the levity afforded by
the parade of clowns that they have come up with. However I dont think that they deserve all the credit
for the current pandemic of the unvaccinated.
Theres a visibility issue.
No one other than healthcare providers could see the pandemics pain, suffering, and end game. No
one!
Even worse visitations from family & close friends were Completely Stopped. The ordinary, usual close
interactions with, support for, and affection towards loved ones were disallowed. We had to helplessly
stand by while they struggled against the virus remotely in enforced anonymity.
Of course I understand all about quarantining and isolation but let us also understand that there is a
cost to that. During the Plague in Europe bodies were thrown out onto the street and the morgue
wagons came through and collected them. Ill bet you would agree that anything approaching that
would take care of the anti-maskers / unvaccinated who are overwhelming our healthcare structure at
the moment.
It is Not a tenable arrangement to continue to shutdown society at large in support of our healthcare
structure. No slight on what had to be done and was done when this first happened, but thats not going
to work long term, going forward. It is Not any kind of a solution.
I hope my comments make the unvaccinated seem less unreasonable to the rest of us. Its not their fault
that the pandemics consequences arent playing out in the community. You tend to deny that which
you cannot see.
There seems to be a clear need to greatly put what the virus does more in front of the community. Not
only would it help us be more focused and attentive but it would lessen diveseness.
Lets talk about how best to do that: open ward hospice, throwing extremist virus deniers out of the
Emergency Room when they show up to be treated for it, greatly relaxing acute/intensive care visiting
restrictions, more in depth reporting on the consequences of what Covid infection does, and
Anything other than limping along the way weve been doing,
