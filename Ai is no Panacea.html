Ai is no Panacea

I don’t know what they are tripping on, these guys selling Artificial Intelligence. In his most wild-eyed moments Ellen Musk councils against saving for retirement b/c you won’t need money in the bountiful beneficent Ai future. People like Bill Gates & Sam Altman talk about work becoming obsolete. Seriously!

I read a really frightening NY Times article about a secretive Ai true believer commune in Berkeley where your opinion about how an autonomous artificial intelligence treats its detractors gateways whether you get in or not.

The Holy Grail of this movement seems to be something called AGI: Artifical General Intelligence.

There actually is an enormous funding arrangement between Microsoft & ChatGPT whose termination hinges on achieving AGI. And that is not the only evidence for how much sway the concept has.

Adherents to the cult will claim that “I don’t get it”. I’m pretty sure that the opposite is the case.

If my progenitor was auto-complete then I’d tune down my arrogance.

I do love technology though. Large Language Models I find attractive as a way of Organizing Knowledge. To me they are like the Dewey Decimal System, Diderot’s first Encyclopedia, or Google’s search engine, useful just as those are. They additionally make knowledge even more accessible.

The notion of a beneficial AGI however troubles me. It seems to somehow stem from belief in its independence. That is mistaken: it will be a Bot doing the bidding of the “Fascist Informational Complex” that built it

The first symptom of infection will be our dependence on it.

Plurality of knowledge and information is The Only Defense !
